01 · Overview

The full BI lifecycle, in one product

Built on enriched Zomato Bangalore data plus a synthetic 200K-row orders fact table (Jan 2023 – Sep 2025). From messy source rows to a decision-ready dashboard.

🧹

Clean

Power Query parses dirty rate text, strips cost separators, and deduplicates 56K rows to one per restaurant.

🧱

Model

A single-fact star schema with single-direction filters and a marked date table.

🧮

Measure

50+ DAX measures — KPIs, time intelligence, what-if & field parameters, a calculation group.

🎨

Design

A hand-built "Indigo Noir" dark theme with sparkline KPIs and drill-through.

03 · Key Insights

What the data revealed

🚨

48% breach the SLA

Nearly half of all orders exceed the 45-minute delivery target — the single biggest operational lever.

🍛

North Indian & Chinese lead

21K and 15.5K listings respectively, ahead of South Indian, Fast Food, and Biryani.

📍

BTM · HSR · Koramangala

The top three delivery hotspots by order volume across the city.

💳

₹825 average basket

Across ~10 orders per customer, with a 10% cancellation rate weighing on revenue.

⭐

3.70 / 5 avg rating

At an average cost of ₹555 for two — a value-heavy, price-sensitive market.

🔁

20K active customers

Repeat-heavy behaviour over a 2.7-year window powers cohort and retention analysis.
